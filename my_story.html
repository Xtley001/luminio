My Journey from Petroleum Engineering to Computer Science

The Beginning

It all started when I embarked on my journey in Petroleum Engineering, a field that promised stability and success. However, as time passed, I found myself at odds with certain aspects of the curriculum, particularly a math course that seemed to steer me off course.

Despite my efforts, my result was released, and I ended up with a zero in the exam, which was unexpected given my very good continuous assessment score. All attempts to have it rectified proved unsuccessful, and eventually, I was advised to consider transferring to another program. The weight of that outcome was more than just the score—it made me question whether this path was truly where I belonged.

The Turning Point

It wasn’t easy to let go of a future that had been carefully planned for me. But after reflecting on what I truly wanted, I realized that my heart lay elsewhere – in the realm of technology, where my passion for problem-solving and creativity could thrive.

Thus, I made the decision to transfer into Computer Science, a choice that aligned perfectly with my dreams and my talents. For the first time, I felt a sense of purpose and clarity. The world of tech beckoned, and I was ready to dive in.

Embracing My New Path

Now, as a Computer Science student, I have a clear goal in sight – to specialize in mobile development with Flutter. I want to create innovative applications that make machine learning accessible to users and bring real-world value to the world of mobile technology.

Through my projects, I’ve explored the depths of mobile development, experimenting with different tools, frameworks, and algorithms. The process is both challenging and rewarding, and I can see how these projects will shape my future.

My AI and ML Projects

Over the course of my journey in AI and machine learning, I’ve had the opportunity to work on several projects that have shaped my understanding of the field and allowed me to apply my skills in meaningful ways. Some of the projects I’ve worked on include:

  • NutriVisual – This project focuses on using AI to analyze nutrition and health data. The goal of NutriVisual is to provide users with detailed insights into their dietary habits by analyzing food images or text input. Leveraging computer vision and machine learning algorithms, NutriVisual can recognize food items and estimate their nutritional value. This is a step toward helping users make healthier decisions based on their specific dietary needs and health goals. The project combines the power of deep learning and data analytics to bridge the gap between nutrition and technology.
  • Skillsyc – Skillsyc is an AI-driven platform designed for skill assessment and improvement. The system uses natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning models to evaluate a user’s skill level in various fields, ranging from coding to communication. Skillsyc provides personalized feedback and offers tailored learning resources to help users improve in the areas they need the most. By assessing performance over time, the platform helps users track their progress, ensuring continuous learning and growth. It aims to make skill development more efficient, data-driven, and accessible.
  • Intercatify – Intercatify is a project focused on improving user interactions through AI. By integrating machine learning models into chatbots and virtual assistants, Intercatify enhances the user experience by making interactions more natural, intuitive, and responsive. The project utilizes sentiment analysis and conversational AI to understand user intent and respond in a way that feels human-like. With applications in customer service, virtual assistants, and personal productivity tools, Intercatify aims to revolutionize how users engage with AI-driven systems, making the experience more seamless and productive.
  • An AI Trading Bot – This project is centered around leveraging machine learning techniques to predict stock market trends and automate trading. The AI trading bot uses historical market data, technical indicators, and machine learning algorithms to predict future price movements. By continuously learning from new market data, the bot adapts its strategies to optimize trading decisions. The goal is to reduce human error in trading and capitalize on market opportunities, making trading more efficient and accessible. This project has allowed me to dive deep into time series analysis and algorithmic trading.
  • Market Scraper – Market Scraper is a tool designed to collect and analyze market data using web scraping techniques. The project focuses on gathering data from various online sources, including financial news websites, stock exchange platforms, and e-commerce sites. By scraping and analyzing this data, Market Scraper helps users track market trends, identify potential investment opportunities, and make informed decisions. The tool incorporates sentiment analysis, price tracking, and trend forecasting to provide a comprehensive view of the market. It’s an example of how AI and automation can streamline data collection and provide valuable insights in real time.

These projects reflect my passion for using AI and machine learning to solve real-world problems, from health and skill development to trading and market analysis. As I continue to learn and grow in the field of mobile development, my goal is to bring these projects to local users, making these powerful tools more accessible and practical for everyday use. The possibilities are endless, and I am excited to continue exploring and developing new solutions in this rapidly evolving field.

Looking Ahead

Although the journey from Petroleum Engineering to Computer Science was unexpected, I now understand that sometimes, it’s the obstacles and setbacks that guide us to where we’re meant to be. The struggles, failures, and changes in direction are all part of the process. Today, I feel more passionate than ever about shaping a career in mobile development, and I am excited to bring my machine learning skills to users around the world.
